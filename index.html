What landscaping services do you offer?

Services may include mowing, edging, landscape maintenance, seasonal cleanups, planting, mulching, bed renovation, drainage improvements, patios, walkways, and complete landscape installations.

Do you offer recurring maintenance?

Yes. Recurring plans can be tailored to the property and season, with visit frequency and included services confirmed in the estimate.

How does the estimate process work?

After a short conversation about the property and goals, we visit the site when needed, confirm the scope, and provide a written estimate before scheduling work.

Do I need to know which plants I want?

No. Tell us how you want the space to look, feel, and function. Recommendations can account for sun, soil, drainage, maintenance preferences, budget, and regional growing conditions.
